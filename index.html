About Me

I have recently transitioned career paths, and am excited about becoming a full-stack web developer. Please take a moment to look through my portfolio!

I have had a somewhat non-traditional career thus far, in that I am a bit of a Jack of All Trades. I have lived in four countries, and have had to be innovative to find work. Every new path has been a "trial by fire", but it has taught me how to think on my feet and be flexible. I hold an MSc in Biomolecular Archaeology from the University of Sheffield (UK), and worked for an archaeological survey company in Ireland. When I returned to the US, I worked for Virginia Commonwealth University--initially sequencing pathogen genomes, then later researching apoptotic mechanisms in gastric and colon cancer. I was well published in both archaeological and scientific journals and am also a published fiction author and illustrator. I look forward to bringing all of my varied experience to my web designs for my clients!
